GreekStrings

GreekStrings are strings composed exclusively of characters that are relevant for ancient Greek: Greek alphabetic characters, accents, breathings or punctuation marks.

GreekStrings are created from Strings by mapping Unicode code points on to ancient Greek characters in one of two mappings:

  • an alphabetic subset of the TLG project's Beta Code
  • a subset of the Unicode ancient Greek range

Beta-code mapping

A GreekString may be created from a case-insensitive String.

Examples

Source String GreekString
MH=NINmh=nin
mh=ninmh=nin
Mh=ninmh=nin
*mh=nin*mh=nin

Unicode Greek mapping

A GreekString may be created from either precombined or combining Unicode codepoints.

Examples

Source String GreekString
μῆνινmh=nin
Μῆνιν*mh=nin
