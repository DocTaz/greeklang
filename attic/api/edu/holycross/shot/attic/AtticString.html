        • static java.lang.String asciiVowelAccToUni(java.lang.String s)

          Replaces ASCII-only vowel-accent combinations not converted by Epidoc transcoder to corresponding encoding in the Unicode Greek range. Since the ASCII characters "E" and "O" correspond only to epsilon and omicron in the ASCII transliteration of literary Ionic Greek, the Epidoc transcoder does not support making them perispomenon. The ASCII sequences are here converted to the equivalent Unicode character followed by the combining form of the perispomenon.

          returns:
          A String Unicode Greek substitutions for "E=" and "O=", in form NFC.
          Parameters:
          s - ASCII string to convert.
